Our Story

Born in the Lanes
of Jaipur

Rang Mahal was born from a deep love for Jaipur's age-old craft traditions. We work directly with artisan families on Maniharon ka Rasta — the legendary bangle lane in the heart of the Pink City.

"Every bangle carries the memory of the hands that made it."

— Artisans of Maniharon ka Rasta, Jaipur

Each piece is shaped from pure natural lac, hand-painted with mineral colors, and finished with intricate stone or meenakari work. No two are exactly alike — that is the beauty of something truly handcrafted.
